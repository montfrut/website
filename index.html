Hello! I am Aniuska Guerra

A proud Venezuelan, mother of a 5-year-old boy, wife, a professional with a degree in Administration, and a passionate entrepreneur in the world of personalized gifts. Welcome to my website, where I offer an exquisite selection of chocolates and chocolate-covered strawberries designed with much love to surprise and delight.

My professional career began in the field of administration, where I acquired skills and knowledge that I now apply to my own business. My love for details and creativity led me to start this project, combining my administrative experience with my passion for chocolates and personalized gifts.

At Montfrut, every detail is carefully crafted and presented to create a unique and memorable experience. I firmly believe that personalized gifts have the power to convey emotions and strengthen bonds, and this is what guides every creation in my business.

What I love most about my work is seeing the joy and surprise on people's faces when they receive one of my creations. I am dedicated to designing products that are not only delicious but also beautiful and meaningful.

Among my achievements, I am proud to have gained the trust and affection of my customers, who appreciate the quality and care I put into every detail. My goal is always to exceed their expectations and offer a service that reflects my commitment to excellence.

My family is a great source of inspiration and motivation in my life. Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new flavor combinations and perfecting my chocolate decoration skills. This passion for innovation and continuous learning is reflected in each of my products.

Thank you for visiting my website. If you have any questions or want to know more about my products or services, feel free to contact me. I am here to help you find the perfect gift for any occasion.
